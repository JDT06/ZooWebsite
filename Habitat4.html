Sea Otter

Photo from https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sea_otter

Sea otters are usually found in kelp beds floating on its back. Sea otters break the shells on their food by hitting it with a stone. Sea otters are the largest otters reaching 100-160cm long and weighing 16-40kg when fully grown. Sea otters mostly eat sea urchins, crabs, and shellfish. When sea otters are sleeping they hold on to each other and kelp to not float away.
